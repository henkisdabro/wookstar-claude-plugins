# Humanizer

Remove AI tell-tales from text. Detects and eliminates 24 patterns of AI-generated writing including corporate jargon, inflated symbolism, pattern repetition, and unnatural structures to make writing sound authentically human.

Based on Wikipedia's "Signs of AI writing" guide maintained by WikiProject AI Cleanup.

## Skills

- **humanizer** - Identifies and removes AI writing patterns from text, replacing them with natural, human-sounding alternatives while preserving meaning and intended tone.
